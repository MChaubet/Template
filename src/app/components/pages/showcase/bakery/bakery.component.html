About us

We create delicious memories
Chee Mooke bakery Ltd. is, at 85, perhaps the oldest surviving on-site bakery in Port of Spain, Trinidad & Tobago. For over eight decades Chee Mooke Bakery Ltd. has maintained the traditional variety of baked products and has delivered consistent quality to many, many loyal customers. Chee Mooke Bakery Ltd. is a familly-run private limited company, whose goals are to provide traditional local bread specialities and to maintain original recipes passed on from the founders.
